What is $ETN ?

#TheRealHabeshaMeme
Welcome to the official website for ET Netsa Coin ($ETN), the premier coin for the ET Netsa Apps™ community project. This site aims to provide comprehensive information about $ETN, including its purpose, tokenomics, and the ways in which our community can engage with and benefit from this innovative digital asset.
Project Overview ET Netsa Coin ($ETN) is a community-driven project designed to demonstrate the dynamics of holding, buying, and selling digital assets. By leveraging live streams on Telegram, we will provide real-time insights into market movements, helping our community understand the impact of different market activities.
Community Engagement - We ask our community in a poll if they wanted to see how tokens prices are affected in a controlled environment and they voted yes. Our community is at the heart of the ET Netsa Coin project. With over 24,000 members, we are committed to fostering a vibrant and active community. We will host live streams on Telegram to monitor market activities and provide real-time buy and sell signals, ensuring transparency and active participation. We will additionally show evidence and live information as to how token Burns affect token price as well as increased liquidity pool this project is an opportunity for our community to learn more about cryptocurrency and how it behaves and maybe make a little bit of extra cash.
